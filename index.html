The addition of vegan caramel sauce adds a rich flavor that complements black tea unbelievably well!

STRAWBERRY

Gong cha's Strawberry Green Milk Tea is a delicious and refreshing beverage that combines the earthy taste of green tea with the sweetness of strawberries and milk.

PANDA

Chains like Gong Cha & T4 have a Panda Milk Tea - it's generally a black or green milk tea with both black (tapioca) pearls, and white (agar) pearls.

COCONUT

MATCHA

Matcha it's a powder made from high-quality green tea leaves originating from China. Its taste is umami, a bit bitterish, slightly astringent, with earthy nuttiness and a few grassy notes. This unique combination of flavors makes it a #1 fan favorite for many.
